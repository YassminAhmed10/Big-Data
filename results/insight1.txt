=== INSIGHT 1: Poverty & Child Labour ===

Pearson Correlation: 0.0081

Interpretation:
  - Weak correlation detected.
  - This does NOT mean poverty is unimportant.
  - Some countries show higher child labour than expected.
  - This suggests factors like weak enforcement.

Average Labour/Poverty Ratio: 1.34

Key Insight:
  Child labour is not explained by poverty alone.
  Structural factors (e.g., enforcement) play a major role.
